## Decisions Made

- Startup should stay visibly `Connecting` until `offerCall(...)` returns and the browser applies the answer; the live call surface starts at the first real listening state.
- Reconnect-tail continuity is more important than preserving tiny recorder scheduling gaps as omitted ranges. Padding those gaps with silence keeps evidence and backend input ordered without inventing new call behavior.
