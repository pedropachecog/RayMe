The missing-chunks acceptance target is that no browser-captured reconnect span
is omitted, including the historical gap `35256-69467ms`.

Local proof artifact:

- `results/03.1-missing-chunks.json`
- Source debug sessions: `phone-calls-missing-chunks.md`,
  `phone-call-transcript-comparison.md`, and
  `phone-call-expected-poem-2026-05-02.md`.
- Forbidden gap: `35256-69467ms`.
- Local status: `passed` for no positive selected-offset gaps in the delayed
  reconnect tail.

Evidence summary:

- Expected-versus-actual transcript comparison shows long turns losing whole
  spans, especially around long passages and pauses.
- Later reconnect diagnostics showed the browser had live microphone audio
  during reconnect while backend STT received only partial audio.
- The most concrete selected gap showed reconnect backfill covering
  `5226-35256ms`, then `69467-99412ms`, with no request covering
  `35256-69467ms`.
- The local unit regression now requires delayed tail selection to start at
  `35256ms` and explicitly not at `69467ms`; the browser regression rejects
  positive selected-offset gaps before applying the replacement answer.
- Local proof preserves the beginning, middle, and ending spans of the captured
  long-turn microphone buffer for downstream STT/writeback. Physical Android
  acceptance remains required after OMEN live gates to prove the same behavior
  on the real phone microphone path.

## Security Hygiene

Evidence and diagnostics in committed files must stay sanitized:

- no API keys, tokens, passwords, or TLS private key material;
- no raw logs or tracebacks;
- no absolute local reference-audio paths;
- no model cache paths;
- no broad diagnostic dumping that creates unbounded log spam.

Every selected triage item carries `requirements`, `context_decisions`,
`evidence_source`, and `phase4_scope: false` so future plans can trace the
stabilization reason without adding Phase 4 scope.
