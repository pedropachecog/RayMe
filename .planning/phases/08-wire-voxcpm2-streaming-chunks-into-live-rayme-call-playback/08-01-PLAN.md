## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-08-01-03 | Elevation of Privilege | VoxCPM2 runtime loading | mitigate | Preserve `require_torch_cuda_runtime("VoxCPM2")`; do not add CPU fallback or package upgrades. |
| T-08-01-04 | Tampering | Streamed chunk enqueue eligibility | mitigate | Emit only non-empty WAV chunks with explicit `chunk_index`, `sample_rate`, and duration metadata. |
| T-08-01-05 | Repudiation | Phase 8 evidence input | mitigate | Expose chunk timing and valid-chunk counts through `TtsAudioChunk.generated_at_ms` for downstream evidence verification. |
