<objective>
Resolve the remaining VoxCPM2 live-call stutter by measuring and enforcing realtime viability, not by increasing buffering or waiting for full TTS completion.

Output: either a VoxCPM2 live profile proven faster than realtime on OMEN, or a live-call guard that refuses/routes non-realtime VoxCPM2 behavior explicitly instead of producing stutter.
</objective>

<user_goal_preservation>
The user must hear continuous call audio during live calls. If VoxCPM2 cannot generate continuous live audio on the target hardware, RayMe must say so through an explicit live-mode decision rather than stuttering or silently pretending the call is fine.
</user_goal_preservation>
